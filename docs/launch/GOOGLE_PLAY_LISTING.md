## Troubleshooting & Tips

### If Description Gets Rejected
- Verify no spelling/grammar errors
- Avoid excessive exclamation marks or emojis
- Remove any unsupported characters
- Check that all URLs are valid and publicly accessible

### If Rated Higher Than "Everyone"
- Review content for violence or inappropriate themes
- Confirm no third-party content is flagged
- Resubmit rating questionnaire if rating changes

### Keyword Optimization
- "Camel" is unique; use it prominently
- "Egypt" and "adventure" are high-search keywords
- "Endless runner" is competitive; include it
- "Free" is essential for discoverability

### Localization Tips
For Arabic-speaking markets (future phase):
- Translate description to Modern Standard Arabic
- Highlight Egypt and MENA representation
- Consider Ramadan-themed events in promotional copy
