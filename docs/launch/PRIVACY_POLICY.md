# Privacy Policy for Desert Dash: Camel Runner

**Effective Date**: 2026-05-13

**Last Updated**: 2026-05-09

---

## 1. Introduction

Desert Dash: Camel Runner (the "App") is a free, mobile endless runner game developed by AI Game Factory. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information from users.

We are committed to protecting user privacy and complying with applicable privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States.

**Key Principle**: The App does NOT require user accounts, logins, or personal identification. All gameplay can be enjoyed without providing any personal data.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

- **No personal identifiable information (PII)**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses
- **No user accounts or logins**: The App does not require account creation or login
- **No social media integration**: We do not request access to social media profiles
- **No location data**: We do not collect precise location or GPS data
- **No camera or microphone access**: The App does not request camera, microphone, or contact list permissions

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The App automatically collects **non-personal, game analytics data** solely for the purpose of improving gameplay and app stability:

#### Game Analytics
- **Gameplay events**: Score, level completion, game duration, feature usage
- **Device information**: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution (for optimization purposes only)
- **Crash and error data**: Technical errors or crashes (to fix bugs and improve stability)
- **Game preferences**: Sound/music settings, graphics quality settings
- **Engagement metrics**: Time in app, feature popularity (to optimize content)

**Note**: None of this data is linked to any personal identity. All data is aggregated and anonymized.

### 2.3 Advertising Data

The App uses third-party advertising networks to display ads. These networks may collect data as described below:

#### AdMob (Google)
- **Advertising identifiers**: Google Advertising ID (Android) or IDFA (iOS)
- **Ad interaction data**: Which ads were shown, clicked, or viewed
- **General device data**: Device type, OS version, approximate location (country/region level only)

**Purpose**: To display relevant ads and measure ad performance. Google's data collection is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### Unity Ads
- **Advertising identifiers**: Unity Ads ID
- **Ad interaction data**: Ad impressions, engagement, completion status (for rewarded ads)
- **Session data**: Session length, content watched

**Purpose**: To serve ads and track reward delivery. Unity's data collection is governed by [Unity's Privacy Policy](https://unity.com/en/legal/privacy-policy)

#### ironSource / LevelPlay (Optional)
- **Advertising ID**: Device advertising identifier
- **Ad performance data**: Impressions, clicks, revenue data
- **Session and engagement metrics**: User session length, content type

**Purpose**: Mediation and optimization of ad delivery. ironSource's data collection is governed by [ironSource's Privacy Policy](https://www.ironsrc.com/privacy/)

### 2.4 Rewarded Video Ads

The App allows players to **voluntarily watch video ads** to earn in-game rewards (revives, coins, etc.). When you choose to watch a rewarded ad:

- The ad network tracks whether you completed watching the entire video
- Ad completion data is used ONLY to determine whether in-game rewards should be granted
- **No additional data** is collected beyond what's described in the Advertising Data section above

**You are never required to watch ads** to progress. All ad-watching is optional and player-initiated.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

1. **Improving the App**: Fixing bugs, optimizing performance, enhancing gameplay based on usage patterns
2. **Displaying Ads**: Serving relevant advertisements through AdMob, Unity Ads, and ironSource
3. **Analytics & Metrics**: Understanding user engagement, feature popularity, and crash trends
4. **Compliance**: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements (including COPPA compliance)

**We do NOT**:
- Sell or share data with third parties for marketing purposes
- Use data for behavioral targeting (except within each ad network's own targeting)
- Merge gaming data with personal information
- Track users across apps or websites

---

## 4. COPPA Compliance (For Users Under 13)

The App is designed to be **child-friendly and COPPA-compliant**. 

### What This Means:
- **No account creation**: Since the App does not require accounts or logins, no personal information about children is collected
- **No persistent user ID**: Players are not tracked individually across sessions
- **Ad safety**: AdMob's content rating is set to "General Audiences (G)" with blocked categories (Gambling, Alcohol, Dating, Adult)
- **No social features**: No commenting, messaging, or social sharing features that could expose children to strangers
- **Parental controls**: Parents can limit rewarded ad watching or ad frequency through device-level restrictions

### If You Are A Parent or Guardian:
If you have concerns about your child's usage of this App, you can:
1. Disable in-app purchasing on your device
2. Use parental control apps to limit ad exposure
3. Supervise your child's gameplay and screen time
4. Contact us (see contact section below) with any privacy concerns

## 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited data collected:

- **Data minimization**: We only collect data necessary for app operation and improvement
- **Encryption in transit**: Data sent to analytics and ad networks is encrypted using HTTPS
- **No sensitive data storage**: Sensitive information (passwords, payment details) is NOT stored locally in the App
- **Device-level protection**: Use your device's built-in security features (lock screen, biometric) to protect your device

**Please note**: While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. Transmission of data over the internet carries inherent risks.

## 8. User Rights and Preferences

### Opting Out of Personalized Ads
- **Android**: Open Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings. Toggle "Personalized ads" OFF.
- **iOS**: Open Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn OFF "Personalized Ads"

### Resetting Your Advertising ID
- **Android**: Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings → "Reset Advertising ID"
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → "Reset Advertising ID"

### Deleting Gameplay Data
Since the App stores gameplay data **locally on your device only** (not on our servers), deleting the App will remove all stored data.

### Right to Access
If you have questions about what data has been collected, please contact us at the address below.
