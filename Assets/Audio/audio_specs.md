# Audio Specifications & Generation Prompts

This document outlines the audio requirements for "Desert Dash: Camel Runner," including background music for each country in the World Tour and all necessary sound effects. These descriptions can be used as prompts for AI audio generation tools.

## Background Music (BGM)

Each country in the World Tour features a unique background track that blends traditional regional instruments with a modern, energetic electronic beat suitable for a fast-paced endless runner.

1.  **Egypt**: Upbeat Middle Eastern fusion. Prominent use of the oud and darbuka, layered over a driving electronic beat. The tone should be adventurous and slightly mysterious.
2.  **Jordan**: Arabian desert vibes. Features a mystical, airy flute melody accompanied by rhythmic, driving percussion and subtle electronic bass.
3.  **India**: Energetic fusion of traditional Indian instruments. A fast-paced sitar melody and complex tabla rhythms, energized by modern electronic synth elements.
4.  **China**: Traditional Chinese instruments meet modern beats. A sweeping erhu melody and rhythmic guzheng plucking, set to a fast, driving electronic drum track.
5.  **Italy**: Upbeat Mediterranean/Italian folk fusion. Lively acoustic guitar or mandolin melodies combined with a bouncy, energetic electronic rhythm.
6.  **Peru**: Andean mountain energy. Fast-paced pan pipes (zampoña) playing a catchy melody over driving, rhythmic tribal drums and a modern bassline.
7.  **France**: Parisian flair with a modern twist. A lively, upbeat accordion melody fused with a fast-paced electronic or electro-swing beat.
8.  **UAE**: Modern Arabic EDM. High-energy electronic dance music incorporating subtle traditional Arabic scales and percussion elements.
9.  **Brazil**: Carnival energy. A fast, rhythmic samba or bossa nova groove, heavily featuring lively percussion and bright electronic synths.
10. **USA**: Energetic rock/pop electronic fusion. A driving, upbeat track featuring electric guitar riffs and a strong, modern pop-electronic beat.

## Sound Effects (SFX)

These sound effects provide crucial audio feedback for player actions and game events.

*   **Jump**: A light, quick "whoosh" sound, like air being sliced.
*   **Slide**: A sandy, gritty "swoosh" sound, simulating sliding on dunes.
*   **Collect Dates**: A soft, satisfying "pop" or light "bloop" sound.
*   **Collect Coins**: A crisp, metallic "ding" or "chime."
*   **Collect Gems**: A bright, magical, resonant sparkle sound.
*   **Power-up Activation**: A magical, echoing chime or ascending synth sweep.
*   **Collision**: A comical, dull "thud" followed immediately by a short, disappointed camel grunt.
*   **Thief Appears**: A sudden, dramatic musical sting or a sharp warning sound (like a quick cymbal crash or synth stab).
*   **Thief Steals Coins**: A negative, descending sound effect, like coins dropping or scattering away.
*   **Escape from Thief**: A short, triumphant, upbeat jingle or ascending chime.
*   **Health Low Warning**: A rhythmic, slightly muffled heartbeat or pulsing sound that increases in tempo.
*   **Health Restored**: A refreshing, splashing water sound or a bright, healing chime.
*   **Game Over**: A comical, descending "sad trombone" or a deflating, winding-down sound.
*   **Milestone Reached**: A bright, encouraging checkpoint chime or short fanfare.
*   **Country Unlocked**: A grand, celebratory fanfare or triumphant orchestral swell.
*   **UI Clicks**: A crisp, dry, sandy "click" or "tap" sound for menu interactions.
